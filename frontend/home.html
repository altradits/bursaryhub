Frequently Answered Metrics

Deep-dive review of system engineering configurations.

How does the data-registry model stop invoice forgery?

Traditional frameworks process document uploads (PDF/images), which are highly susceptible to fabrication[cite: 5]. BursaryFund Hub shifts to a data-registry model where institutions publish immutable, audited Fee Master structures before allocations occur[cite: 6, 8]. Incoming claims are programmatically validated against these guidelines, discarding any mismatched requests[cite: 10, 63].

What specific technical prerequisites do grantors require?

Grantor administrative nodes require standard web browsers with internet connectivity, protected by Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) controls[cite: 23]. Financially, they must provide traditional wire transfer mechanisms (EFT/RTGS) to clear localized assets into the smart contract escrow environment[cite: 24, 56].

Can a student route a payout to a personal account?

No. The underlying smart contract structures are restricted to multi-party rules[cite: 44, 51]. Upon student signature validation via secure SMS OTP, funds are moved out of escrow and transferred strictly to the whitelisted institution's digital wallet and bank account[cite: 66, 69, 70].
